When you need a large item courier service

If you’re transporting items such as furniture which are too heavy for you to lift safely by yourself or too bulky to fit in a car or small van, you’ll need the services of a large item courier

Such specialists will be able to move and deliver your outsized heavy goods or cargo throughout the UK, whether you’re a small business needing bulky items delivering to another business, or an individual with a large item for delivery to a residential address.

The sort of goods suited for large item delivery

A large item delivery specialist will be familiar with handling a wide range of heavy and bulky items including tables, sofas, gym equipment, garden furniture, wardrobes and even pool tables. Certain items will need careful handling, such as antique furniture and pianos, but all can be collected and transported efficiently and cheaply if you find the right courier for the job

Finding a courier to deliver your large, or heavy items

If you live in one corner of the country and need a large, bulky item delivering to another, you'll immediately think of couriers but, with no experience of couriers you'll assume it’s major hassle to find reliable courier services to transport your goods halfway around the country. And it’ll cost a fortune. Not so. With Any-Movers.com all you have to do is enter the details of what you need delivering and we’ll do the rest.

Just like eBay, once you’ve entered the details of that big item you want shifting, courier companies who can deliver will submit bids to transport your bulky items. As the companies can see what each other is bidding, the price is bound to go down and you’ll get the cheapest quote possible for that large item you can hardly even lift.

Keeping the costs affordable

Once you’ve entered the details of your job onto the website finding cheap couriers for your large item deliveries is easy – you do nothing! Whilst you’re sitting back with a cup of tea congratulating yourself on the fact that someone actually bought that huge ugly wardrobe you inherited when you moved, we’re doing all the work for you.

Large item delivery services will be busy submitting quotes for your job. Companies with vans who are returning empty or travelling half full will jump at the chance to recuperate some costs by offering you their spare capacity at low price, which means everyone is happy. Your large item gets delivered at a reasonable price and the courier avoids driving empty, all because we’ve matched you with somebody going there already

Free, no obligation quotes for delivering big or bulky items

The great thing Any-Movers.com is that it’s free for you to use and you don’t have to accept any of the quotes offered. As the bids come in, we recommend you check out the company profile of each supplier and read up on customer feedback too. That way you can make an informed decision. All this is reassuring if you’ve never used a large item courier specialist before.

Picking the best large item courier for your needs

It’s entirely up to you what sort of service you go for. If you run a small business and a vital piece of machinery has broken down you’ll want to know that a courier will get those heavy parts delivered within hours. If you’ve bought a corner sofa that will fit perfectly in your lounge, you’ll want to know you can get it moved for less than the cost you paid for it

As you browse the quotes, bear in mind what is most important to you. Will it be collected and delivered by the same driver? Is the van a Luton style with a tail-lift to make loading that sideboard easier? Is the company geared up to handling a baby grand piano? If it’s a sole driver is there someone the other end who can help unload or will it require a two person delivery service? You might also want to think about delivery times and if there is insurance included in with the cost.
