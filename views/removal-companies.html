Doing removals the 'easy way'

Moving house is one of the most stressful things you can do, take some of that stress away by using Any-Movers.com to find reliable removal companies, whether you are moving within the UK or internationally.

Rather than you scouring removal websites or ringing numerous companies for quotes, AnyMovers.com can do this for you, not only saving you time but also money. The quotes we collect are usually lower than contacting removal firms direct for a quote. Companies use our site to maximise capacity of their vehicles. With most removal jobs at least one leg of the overall journey, the vehicle will be empty, this means that companies providing removal services can offer a competitive price when they are able to fulfil your needs and theirs.

Simply enter your moving date, what type of property you're moving from and to and whether you need help packing and loading or not. You can also fill in additional information, including what needs moving from each room in your home for a more comparative price. Home removal companies will then bid on your removal job (a bit like a reverse auction) and you are then able to choose the cheapest quote.

The different removal services available

When using Any-Movers.com whether for national or international removals, you can specifically choose what service you require when you move house. You can choose from a number of different options, from the basic pick up and drop off to packing and unpacking services. With packing and unpacking services, the home removal companies supply boxes and packing material, which means one less thing for you to think about.

By supplying information on the property type you are moving from and to and an inventory of items which need moving, it will allow us to find the right removal company to avoid additional cost pitfalls. It also means that the home removal companies can make sure they have enough workers and the right equipment with them

Comparing quotes from removal companies

Once you have requested quotes, when they come in you can compare each one to find the one which is suitable for what you need. The more precise information you supply in the initial quote page will ensure that you get the exact quote for what you require. On our site you are able to review and compare your quotes; the removal companies on Any-Movers.com their own profile page where you can see other customer reviews.

You can compare costs, by pricing up self-hire vans, but don’t forget to take into consideration your time and extra costs for boxes and packing. You will find quotes supplied on our site are cheaper and of course you can save yourself time and stress.

Don’t forget to consider other removal related matters

Insurance – Any company which provides home removals must have Goods in Transit & Liability insurance. It is important to ensure that the cover provided is sufficient for your possessions. If it isn’t cover can be increased at an extra cost. You could be liable for extra costs if anything happens during transportation.

Trade Association Membership – There is no legal obligation for a removal company to be members of The British Association of Removers (BAR), however those that are have to adhere to a certain level of professionalism and service. Choosing removal companies that are members of BAR, means that you can trust that there will be a higher standard of service to expect.
